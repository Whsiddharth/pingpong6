AI PINGPONG








Instructions

  1. Move your cursor up and down with the ball to hit it! If ou fail to hit it the computer gets a point. If you hit it, you get a point!
  2. When the computer reaches 4 points, it's game over for you and the computer wins.
  3. You can always restart game by clicking the "restart" button.

Have fun!
